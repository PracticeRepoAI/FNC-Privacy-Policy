## Introduction

FillnChill (“FNC”, “we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a simple, offline-first household pantry tracker published by **Bharat Apps Lab**. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it on your Android device.

We designed FNC so your pantry data stays **on your phone**. We do not operate user accounts, cloud sync, or advertising networks.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers.**

All information you enter in the app is saved **locally on your device** using on-device storage, including:

- Pantry items (names, quantities, units, categories, stock status)
- Shopping list items
- Your display name (optional, set during onboarding)
- Notification preferences (reminder times, alert settings)
- App settings and onboarding completion state

This data never leaves your device unless **you** choose to share something outside the app (for example, using your phone’s Share feature).

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do **not**:

- Create user accounts or require sign-in
- Sync your data to the cloud or our servers
- Sell, rent, or trade your information
- Use analytics or advertising SDKs to track you across apps or websites
- Access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location

---

## Notifications

FNC can send **optional local notifications** on your device, such as:

- Daily pantry check-in reminders
- Low-stock or out-of-stock alerts at times you choose

These notifications are scheduled and displayed **on your device only**. They are not sent through our servers.

To use notifications, the app may request permission to:

- Post notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)
- Schedule reminders at specific times, including when the app is closed (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`, `USE_EXACT_ALARM`)
- Reschedule reminders after device restart (`RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`)
- Wake the device briefly so a reminder can appear (`WAKE_LOCK`)

You can deny notification permission or turn off alerts in the app at any time. The rest of the app works without notifications.

---

## How We Use Information

Because all data stays on your device, we do not use your information for profiling, marketing, or automated decision-making. The app uses your locally stored data only to:

- Show your pantry and shopping list
- Calculate stock status (Enough / Low / Out)
- Schedule local reminders and alerts you enable

---

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with third parties.**

There is no backend service receiving your pantry information. No data is shared with advertisers, data brokers, or social networks.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it by:

- Deleting items inside the app
- Clearing the app’s storage in Android Settings
- Uninstalling FillnChill

When you uninstall the app or clear its data, your pantry information is removed from that device. We cannot recover it because we never stored it on our servers.

---

## Children’s Privacy

FillnChill is a general household utility app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect data on our servers, no child data is transmitted to us.

---

## Security

Your data is protected by your device’s operating system and app sandbox. We recommend keeping your phone locked with a PIN, password, or biometric security.
